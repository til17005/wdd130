About Water Snake Rapids!

In business since 1903 when a deal was struck with the local natives for passage across their land. We would purchase supplies from them and in return we were allowed safe travel to enter the snakes mouth; the striking entrance to a fantastic white water journey. We invested in wooden canoes (death traps, I call them) from those same natives. I am positive they were laughing the whole way.

In those times we catered to trappers and the most greedy of gold diggers. They would travel those serpentine waterways hoping to strike it rich. More often they found disappointment due to losing all of their gear when those death traps flipped. Ahhh, those were the days!

Nowadays, we use the latest in high tech white water equipment. Safety is our priority. Whether you join a short outing, or mutil-day event, you can trust we will bring you home safe and sound.

History

Water Snake Rapids began way back when my dad was up north panning for gold. Like so many shmucks before him he lost his shirt trying to find success in a dying trade. Although, he did have a good head on his shoulders and the proof is in the continued success of this business. He was down on his luck with no one to lean on. He had been fighting a fever for three days when he came across a small native village. At first he thought they were going to kill him, but to his surprise they scraped him off the ground and nursed him back to health.

He stayed with them for four months and that is where he learned how they traveled the river. They called the opening between two ridges the snakes mouth. The ridges look like they have fangs that lower into the rapids. After some brutal negotiations and marrying the cheifs daughter in a ritual he would never speak about, he came to an arrangement to start this rafting business.
