Utah

Utah is a fantastic place to live. It is full of great people and headquarters of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. It also has a variety of beautiful country and landscapes to explore. Northern Utah offers the High Uintas, which offer a mix of pine and aspen forests with many lakes. Southern Utah offers red rocks of Bryce Canyon, Escalante, Moab, Goblin Valley and more. Western Utah offers a more broad desert landscape with an area of sand dunes. Go East and you travel through many layers of the grand Rocky Mountains.
